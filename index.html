About Us

Programmers Club at SRIT was established in the year 2019. It aims to establish a coding culture on campus, reaching every student passionate about coding. This helps them immensely during placements and can also bring laurels to the institution through various national and international programming contests. The Club’s main motto is Empowering Knowledge. Programmers Club at SRIT will help students to get the updates of programming. It also contains all the relevant details about the club right from its initiation. It consists of contact information form, a blog to showcase the problems related to coding, previous month contest questions and also a payment gateway integration to pay fees for campus hackathons.
