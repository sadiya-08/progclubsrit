about us

Programming skills are essential to students in order to test and enhance their problem solving and analytical skills. Programmers Club aims to establish a coding culture on campus, reaching every student passionate about coding. This helps them immensely during placements and can also bring laurels to the institution through various national and international programming contests. The club’s main motto is Empowering Knowledge
